About

Fidil is an online Irish Traditional Music resource which delivers content in a bitesized, aesthetically pleasing manner which appeals to the hobbyist musician. While it is hoped that in time this website will continue to develop and find it’s place as a valuable asset and contributor to the Irish Traditional Music community, at this point in time the purpose of the creation of this website is primarily educational - it has been created as an exercise for the developer’s portfolio in full-stack development as well as design. As such it is not intended to be exhaustive at this point in time. Content for each of the articles have been sourced from Wikipedia, and images have been licensed from Shutterstock. All other design elements are the developer’s own intellectual property.
